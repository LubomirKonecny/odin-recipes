Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, salt, yeast and oil to make pizza dough.
  2. Let it rise.
  3. Mix tomato paste, basil and salt to make tomato sauce.
  4. Put it on pizza dough.
  5. Add cheese on top.
  6. Put it to oven and let it bake.
  7. Take it out and serve.
